ABOUT US

Who are we


Because our neighborhood does not have social gathering places like a market, a library and a cultural center - Vestigium breathes as a SOCIAL CENTER in the hands of citizens and organizes a little - all of that!! You can bring books to our FREE LIBRARY - which serves for books to be exchanged and read, not to sit and gather dust. We encourage the love of reading.

What we offer

Eco market

We gather around the little OPGovci, get to know them, ask them everything we are interested in about their work and farming methods, drink coffee, eat breakfast and buy healthy, freshly picked, organically grown fruits and vegetables.

Green baskets

Baskets are ordered from Thursday to Sunday via the links, and are picked up directly from the manufacturer on MONDAY EXACTLY AT 6PM! With the manufacturer, you can agree on the size of the surprise basket and a subscription for several months, in this way you support him, and he rewards you with excess products.
