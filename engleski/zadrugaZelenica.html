Cooperative zelenica

The program is created in cooperation with members, citizens, artists, creatives. Which means that - if you have a suggestion, contact us at zadruga.zelenica@gmail.com The ZDC will be open during the events, which you can follow on our Facebook page or through the newsletter.

In ZDC you can borrow a tool or a book from our library, organize an event, promotion, celebration. We are currently working on projects such as a community chicken coop and a shared orchard, and in our long-term plans we also see a shared housing project - the future Eco Village.

Donations of 50-100 HRK are welcome to cover the costs of materials, organization and performers.
